Mozzarella Cheese Sticks Recipe

Description

Homemade mozzarella sticks that have an amazing taste but also cheesy outcome.

This recipe is by Lili Zimmermann found in the allrecipes website.

Ingredients

Ingredients listed is for 5 servings.

  • Two eggs
  • Two cups milk
  • One and one-half cups Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • Ten egg roll wrappers
  • Ten mozzarella string cheese sticks
  • One quart vegetable oil for frying
