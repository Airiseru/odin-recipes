Grilled Salmon Recipe

Description

A grilled salmon recipe that has a unique but excellent taste. The marinade gives the salmon a more pleasurable experience to the mouth.

This recipe is by Meyerkl2 found in the allrecipes website.

Ingredients

Ingredients listed is for 4 servings

  • One-third cup soy sauce
  • One-fourth cup orange juice concentrate
  • Two tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Two tablespoons tomato sauce
  • One teaspoon lemon juice
  • One-half teaspoon prepared mustard
  • One tablespoon minced green onion
  • One minced clove garlic
  • One-half teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • Four salmon steaks (1 inch thick)
  • One tablespoon olive oil
