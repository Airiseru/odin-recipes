Buttermilk Oatmeal Waffle Recipe

Description

Great-tasting waffle for breakfast and picky eaters.

This recipe is by Mike Mader found in the allrecipes website.

Ingredients

  • Two and one-half cups all-purpose flour
  • One and one-half cups rolled oats
  • Three tablespoons white sugar
  • Two tablespoons baking powder
  • One and one-half tablespoons salt
  • Four eggs, separated
  • One and one-half cups buttermilk
  • One cup milk
  • One-half cup melted butter
  • One and one-half teaspoons vanilla extract
